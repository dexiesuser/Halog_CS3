AM by Arctic Monkeys

  • Released in 2013, the AM album by the Arctic Monkeys brought rock-and-roll to the mainstream once again.
  • With catchy guitar riffs and a hypnotizing beat with every album track, AM succeeded in engaging listeners to appreciate the rock genre.
  • Top hits like "Do I Wanna Know" and "Why'd You Only Call Me When You're High?" gained acclaim and praise from the general crowd, topping charts on streaming sites like Spotify.
  • In my own experience, this was one of the first rock albums that made me fall deep into the rabbit hole of the rock genre, earning a precious place in my heart.
