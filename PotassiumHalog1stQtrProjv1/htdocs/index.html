Arctic Monkeys: Influence of the Modern Poster Rock Band

A Brief Introduction

Arctic Monkeys arose from the sludges of Sheffield, England in 2005, literate garage rock emanating with every riff they played. The band soon became a househoold name in the UK, but were still considered an obscure indie band in the US. From spreading their CDs for free to selling out tickets in grand arenas, how exactly did the band captivate the ears of many music enthusiasts?
